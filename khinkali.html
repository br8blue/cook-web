What is Khinkali?

Khinkali is a popular traditional dish in the country of Georgia, not to be confused with the state in the US. It is similar to a dumpling, as it is made of twisted dough. It is also stuffed with cottage cheese, meat, and some vegetables. The most commonly used meats for it include beef and pork, and people also put in mashed potato and mushrooms.
